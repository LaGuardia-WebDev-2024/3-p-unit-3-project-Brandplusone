All about dogs

The dog is a pet animal and is considered to be one of the most obedient animals.
There are various kinds of dogs in the world, and some of them are known to be very
friendly while some are dangerous. There are various species of dogs that we come across,
and some have slippery shiny skin, while others have rough skin. Dogs are carnivorous
animals and they like eating meat
